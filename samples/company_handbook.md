# Acme Robotics — Customer Handbook

## About Acme Robotics

Acme Robotics was founded in 2019 in Lisbon, Portugal. We design and
manufacture small-form-factor service robots for the hospitality
industry. Our flagship product line, the **Atlas series**, is deployed
in over 120 hotels across Europe and the Middle East.

Our headquarters are at Rua das Janelas Verdes 84, 1200-690 Lisboa,
Portugal. We have regional offices in Dubai (UAE) and Cairo (Egypt).

## Product Lineup

### Atlas-1

The Atlas-1 is our entry-level concierge robot, intended for boutique
hotels with up to 60 rooms. It supports voice interaction in English,
Arabic, French, Portuguese, and Spanish. Battery life is 8 hours of
continuous operation, and a full recharge takes 90 minutes.

The Atlas-1 retails for **€7,400** and ships within 4 to 6 weeks of
order confirmation. It is covered by a 24-month limited warranty.

### Atlas-2 Pro

The Atlas-2 Pro is built for larger venues (120+ rooms). It can carry
trays up to 12 kg, navigates multi-floor buildings via Wi-Fi-controlled
elevator integration, and supports the same five languages as the
Atlas-1 plus Mandarin Chinese and German.

The Atlas-2 Pro retails for **€18,900**. Lead time is 8 to 10 weeks.
It comes with a 36-month warranty and free firmware updates for the
lifetime of the device.

## Returns and Refunds

We accept returns within **30 calendar days** of delivery for any
reason, provided the device is in as-new condition with all original
packaging. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within
10 business days of the device arriving at our Lisbon warehouse.

Custom-configured units (those with engraved branding or non-standard
firmware loadouts) are non-returnable.
